About Our Hotel

Welcome to Grand Horizon, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of the city. Our hotel offers an unparalleled experience with world-class amenities, exquisite dining options, and impeccable service.

Since opening our doors in 2005, we have been committed to providing our guests with memorable stays. Each of our 250 rooms and suites has been meticulously designed to offer the perfect blend of elegance and modern convenience.
